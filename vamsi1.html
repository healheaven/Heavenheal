Reason for our project

child labour and Money are the major problems faced in india. Children have a right to education,freedon, health. On basis on this we start a program called HEAL (health and Education for All). Our aim is give free education to all orphans, unpriviliged students, poor back grounds.



Founder

Dr. Koneru satya prasad

Dr. koneru satya prasad

Our heal founder Dr. Koneru satya prasad. He is so kind hearted person who loves children so much. he had won our Pride in Peterborough lifetime achievement award - volunteering 5,000 miles away at the Indian village he is building for 1,000 orphan and needy children. Then he decided to launch HEAL program. Later in 2013 the Heal paradise villages started in thotapalli near ganavaram.

The village encompasses school buildings, housing for the children and their carers, health, sports and arts facilities, a care home for the elderly, an institute for the blind, and buildings for community use.
